Description

lasagna is made of three parts

  • The meat sauce
  • The white sauce
  • Assembling and baking

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Lasagna sauce
  • Lasagna sheets

5 easy steps to make the perfect lasagna

  1. Cook the Meat
  2. Place the lasagna sheets in hot water while the meat is cooking
  3. Allow for the meat to turn brown and the lasagna sheet to become soft
  4. In a trinomial order, add the lasagna sheets covering the bottom of the glass plate, add the meat and then add the lasagna sauce. repeat this again until you run out of room or Ingredients
  5. Last but not least, ENJOY!
